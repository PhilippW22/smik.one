ON THE FOLLOWING PAGES YOU CAN GET AN INSIGHT INTO THE VERSATILITY OF MY ART. HAVE A LOOK AND ENJOY! IF YOU'VE AN IDEA OR REQUEST, DON'T HESITATE AND GET IN TOUCH WITH ME:

Murals offers many possibilities to create unique works of art from large plain walls. I have been able to paint a few so far. Check them out at my gallery.

Graffiti has become an important part of my life. Over the years I have left marks in different sizes, shapes and colours on a wide variety of surfaces in different environments.

On my journey I meet a lot of interesting people and institutions. Signboards are a great way to create attention for something, even if there isn't a suitable wall for a mural.

Sketching is a big part of my work. Most ideas find their way onto walls or signs - others find their way onto other projects. In my gallery you will find an overview of some of my sketches.
